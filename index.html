Phishing Campaign Simulation

Date: April 2025

This was a controlled phishing campaign conducted to assess organizational awareness around phishing threats using GoPhish.

Objective

Test users' ability to identify suspicious emails and determine the click/open rate of a crafted phishing link.

Infrastructure

  • Vultr VPS running Ubuntu
  • GoPhish server hosted behind an Nginx reverse proxy
  • Custom domain with email aliases

Email Details

A professional-looking email was crafted to resemble IT support, containing a link to a spoofed login portal.

Landing Page

The landing page mimicked a real login form and tracked user interactions. All data collected was non-sensitive and part of the simulation.

Redacted Target Info

The organization name has been redacted for confidentiality. All activities were authorized and performed ethically.

Results

The results are being analyzed and will inform improved training and alerting procedures going forward.

For educational and professional demonstration only. All simulations were conducted in a legal and authorized manner.
