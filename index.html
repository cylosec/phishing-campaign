Phishing Campaign Simulation

Date: April 2025

This was a controlled phishing campaign conducted to assess organizational awareness around phishing threats using GoPhish.

Campaign Summary

Detail Info
Target Audience Internal staff at [REDACTED]
Delivery Method Email (via trusted-looking IT support alias)
Landing Page Custom spoofed login form
Goal Measure click rate and login attempts

Infrastructure

  • Vultr VPS running Ubuntu
  • GoPhish server hosted behind an Nginx reverse proxy
  • Custom domain with email aliases

Email & Page Details

A professionally written email posed as an internal IT message. The link led to a spoofed login page that logged credentials without storing any real passwords or sensitive data.

Ethical Disclosure

All testing was fully authorized by the organization. No personal data was stored or exposed. The target organization has been redacted to maintain confidentiality.

Next Steps

The results are being used to improve user training and security posture. A final report has been shared with stakeholders to guide remediation and awareness efforts.

For educational and professional demonstration only. All simulations were conducted in a legal and authorized manner.
